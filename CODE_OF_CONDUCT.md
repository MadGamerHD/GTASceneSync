GTASceneSync 2.0.0 Code of Conduct
Last Updated: [Insert Date]

The GTASceneSync 2.0.0 Blender Addon is an open-source tool designed to help the GTA modding community create amazing projects. To ensure a welcoming and collaborative environment, all users and contributors must adhere to the following Code of Conduct.

1. Respect and Inclusivity
Treat everyone with respect, regardless of skill level, background, or opinions.
Harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior of any kind will not be tolerated.
Avoid offensive language, personal attacks, or trolling.
2. Collaboration and Support
Provide constructive feedback when discussing issues, suggestions, or feature requests.
Be patient with others, especially those new to modding or Blender.
Share knowledge and resources to help the community grow.
3. Responsible Use
Use the GTASceneSync addon for legal purposes only.
Do not use the addon to create or distribute content that violates copyright or other intellectual property laws.
4. Reporting Issues and Bugs
Submit bug reports or feature requests respectfully through the official GitHub repository or contact channels.
Provide clear and detailed descriptions of issues to help with resolution.
Avoid spamming or repeatedly posting the same issue.
5. Contribution Guidelines
Ensure that any contributions (e.g., code, documentation, or assets) align with the goals and standards of the addon.
Respect the development team's decisions and priorities.
Clearly document any contributions to help others understand and utilize them.
6. Respect Development Decisions
The addon is maintained and updated as time and resources allow. Be understanding if updates are delayed or certain features take longer to implement.
Constructive feedback is welcome, but repeated demands or entitlement will not be tolerated.
7. Be Professional
Represent yourself and the community in a professional manner when engaging with external forums, platforms, or social media.
Refrain from posting false or misleading information about the addon.
8. Reporting Violations
If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to MadGamerHD through the following contact channels:

YouTube: bradzandmaxplays
Consequences of Violations
Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

Warnings or temporary bans from community platforms.
Permanent removal from the community in severe cases.
Revocation of access to the addon or its associated resources.
Acknowledgment
By using or contributing to the GTASceneSync addon, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct. Let’s work together to maintain a friendly and productive community!
